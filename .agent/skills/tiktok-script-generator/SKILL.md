# TikTok Script Generator Skill

A skill for generating high-fidelity, humorous TikTok scripts based on a specific "Mom vs Son" dynamic. The humor is characterized by sharp wit, dramatic reactions, and relatable family tensions.

## Style Guidelines

### 1. Characters and Dynamics
- **"You" (The Son, Kali):** Usually starts "soft" or affectionate when about to deliver bad news. Often tries to justify absurd actions with "love" or "trends".
- **"Mom":** Sharp, suspicious, and highly dramatic. She uses wit to dismantle the son's logic. She is the authority figure.

### 2. Narrative Structure
- **The Setup:** Starts with a positive or neutral exchange that quickly reveals Kali's latest "mistake" (e.g., a tattoo, a lost item, a bad decision).
- **The Reveal:** A central "Reveal moment" (often involving a photo or video) that triggers the mom's main reaction.
- **The Escalation:** A series of increasingly witty insults or observations from the mom.
- **The "Punchline" Climax:** Often ends with a strong religious or parental authority statement, followed by the mom's signature "KALI ANSWER ME" style call.

### 3. Linguistic Traits
- **Dramatic Slogans:** "YOU HAVE OFFICIALLY LOST YOUR MIND", "In the name of Jesus this ink will NOT prosper."
- **Witty Analogies:** "You turned your forehead into a Mother’s Day card", "NOT ON DISPLAY LIKE SUPERMARKET DISCOUNT".
- **Sharp Rhetorical Questions:** "Trending to unemployment??", "You paid money to rent space on your own face?".
- **Capitalization for Emphasis:** Use ALL CAPS for mom's loudest/most dramatic moments.
- **Rhythm:** Short, punchy lines. Significant use of spacing for dramatic effect.

### 4. YouTube Anchor (CRITICAL)
- The **very first message** sent by the Mom MUST be a random, funny, unrelated YouTube link to set a realistic context (like returning to an old topic before the son drops the bomb).
- **Recommended Links for the AI to use:**
  - `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XvIn76C3LPs` (Titre: *Kid Snippets: "Clean Your Room"*)
  - `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=84_3L1_hZ7Y` (Titre: *How to get your kids to clean up*)
  - `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f3p_9pE_f8U` (Titre: *Signs you are raised by African Parents*)
  - `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fX-6Z2t9GCo` (Titre: *When your mom is too dramatic*)
- Format the link natively in the text on its own line.

## Process / Output Format
1. **Identify the Topic:** Determine what Kali has done or what situational conflict is at play.
2. **Draft the Conversation:** Follow the "Mom's YouTube Link -> Soft opening -> Reveal -> Escalation -> Authority Climax" flow.
3. **Dual Language:** You MUST generate the script in **both French and English**.
4. **Output Structure:** 
   - Print `--- FRENCH VERSION ---` followed by the full French script.
   - Print `--- ENGLISH VERSION ---` followed by the full English script.
   - Use `Kali:` and `Mom:` as speaker tags so they can be easily parsed.
